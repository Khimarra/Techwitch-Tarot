Techwitch Tarot

Tarot is not about fortune telling, but rather about revealing one's inner thoughts and desires. There are two basic types of reading: open readings, and question readings. Open readings are often used as a way to receive messages. Question readings, as the name suggests, are meant to help us answer a question or make a decision.
